Service Description

Our most popular detailing package not only includes a thorough cleaning of both the inside and outside, but also the application of non-greasy conditioner to all interior leather and plastics, restoring their shine and preventing fading. Non-leather seats receive fabric protector, which help prevent future stains. On the outside, an upgraded wax is used to give maximum shine and protecting that will last for months

Exterior:

- Foam bath/ hand wash with conditioning car soap - Claybar service to remove stubborn contaminants - Deep Clean Wheels/ Wheel wells - Apply Ceramic wax - Clean Windows - Dress tires - Black plastic trim restored - Chrome polished
